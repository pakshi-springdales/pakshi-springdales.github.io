Welcome to Pakshi's Springdales

Pakshi's Springdales Senior Secondary School is a successful, well-ordered and caring co-educational school. We are proud of our successes in academic and co-curricular fields and are committed to the highest levels of achievement for pupils of all abilities. The school has excellent facilities and resources, as well as school with wide focus for effective teaching and learning.

We respect and value all faiths, beliefs and cultures. Our success in achieving high standards of teaching and learning is underpinned by an emphasis on decency, good behavior and self-discipline. We have high expectations of our students and aim to:

  • Support each student's spiritual, moral and social development
  • Identify, nurture and monitor individual talents and skills so that all students can achieve their full potential
  • Provide a secure, happy and purposeful learning environment in which the highest academic standards can be achieved and maintained

Our Aims

We are committed to supporting each student's spiritual, moral and social development. Our dedicated team identifies, nurtures and monitors individual talents and skills, ensuring all students achieve their full potential in a supportive and encouraging environment.
